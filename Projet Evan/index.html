About my startup: It would be a clothing brand associated with the Ca' store association and would be called K matters, in reference to the K of Black Lives Matter and the K of Kedge. The goal of this project is to create an ethical product for the students and for the school's international image to show that Kedge students care about humanitarian causes and that the school also follows this path. Besides the K matters brand would develop locally in and around Bordeaux. In the current context of economic and health crisis, the social dimension takes on capital importance with an awareness of what really matters, i.e. what is human and not economic or political. Hence the birth of this project, which has a major social dimension.
